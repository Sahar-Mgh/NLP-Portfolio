# Paper presentation — Hypothesis-Only Baselines in NLI

Portfolio deliverable #1 (paper presentation).

**Paper:** Poliak, Naradowsky, Haldar, Rudinger & Van Durme (2018).
*Hypothesis-Only Baselines in Natural Language Inference.* *SEM 2018.

**Slides:** [`hypothesis_only_nli_presentation.pptx`](hypothesis_only_nli_presentation.pptx) — 13 slides.

The paper shows that NLI models can often predict the label from the **hypothesis alone** —
evidence of annotation artifacts in the datasets — and recommends always reporting a
hypothesis-only baseline alongside the full model. This directly motivates the claim-only /
evidence-only baselines used in the [capstone](../capstone/).
